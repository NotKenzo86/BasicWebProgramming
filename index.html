Airbus A350

The Airbus A350 is a widebody airliner manufactured by Airbus. Starting flights from 2013, it is the world's most advanced airliner. It featured 2 variants, A350-900 (ICAO: A359) and the A350-1000 (iCAO: A35X). The airliner can fly from 7800 to 9800 nautical miles. The A350 is 54% made of carbon fibers (CFPB) with two trent 1000 engines. it is designed to replace the A340 family, Boeing 777-200/200ER and compete with the 777X family and larger variants of the Boeing 787 Dreamliner.
